## Core thesis

> A workspace is the boundary of a Sim system. For teams and customers, it becomes the app environment: resources, credentials, members, deployments, and handoff all live there.

This module is more partner / enterprise-oriented. The basic-user version is short; the deeper treatment belongs in [partner certification](/academy/partner-certification).

<Callout type="info">
A partner isn't just someone who can build a workflow. They set up a workspace the customer can actually use, govern, and maintain.
</Callout>
