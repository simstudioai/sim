## Why knowledge bases

After workflows and tables, systems need the **unstructured-memory** layer. Tables handle structured records. Knowledge Bases handle documents, notes, policies, manuals, and websites — information that should be semantically searchable by agents and workflows.

## Core thesis

> Knowledge Bases are Sim's searchable memory layer.

<Callout type="info">
Use a knowledge base when the question is "find relevant information from a body of documents." Tables hold structured rows; files hold artifacts; knowledge bases make document *contents* searchable.
</Callout>
